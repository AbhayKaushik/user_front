How It Works(Pharmacists)

Get your shop registered on our portal

Get unused medicines from nearby people

Sell these medicines too

Increase customer base

Can sell the excess medicines to Hospitals and NGOs

How It Works(Users)

Download the app from the play store

Take a pic of your medicine with the expiry date. The app records the expiry date and keeps track of it.

Notifies when the medicine is about to expire.

Select your pharmacy shop from the map and put up an offer for the transaction.

Give your medicine to the selected pharmacist and receive in-app credits.

Buy more medicines later from the credits earned.
